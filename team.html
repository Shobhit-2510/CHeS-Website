Message from the ChES Faculty President

A quick note from leadership on where we’re headed and how we support our members.

I wanted to take a moment to thank our outgoing members and welcome new members to what promises to be another exciting year for the Chemical Engineering Society. Our field is one of constant innovation and problem-solving, and the society is here to support you every step of the way.

We'll be hosting a variety of events this year, including guest lectures from industry professionals and alumni, workshops to hone your practical skills, industrial visits to observe and understand industry practices, and social gatherings to connect with your peers. These events are designed to complement your academic studies and provide you with a deeper understanding of what a career in chemical engineering truly looks like.

Prof. Vikrant Verma
Faculty President, ChES
