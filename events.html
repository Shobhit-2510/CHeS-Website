Events

From social gatherings like Freshers' Night, Chem-E-Night, and Traditional Evenings to high-impact academic initiatives, research expos, case study competitions, workshops, and industry visits, ChES delivers year-round opportunities for learning, networking, and growth. Our events blend fun, skill-building, and career development, creating a vibrant, connected Chemical Engineering community.

Social Gatherings

Connect, celebrate, and build lasting friendships within the ChES community.

Social & Cultural
Kickoff

Freshers' Night

Where every ChemE journey begins.

Freshers' Night marks the first step into the vibrant Chemical Engineering community at IIT Delhi. Organized by seniors, the evening welcomes the incoming batch with performances, laughter, and shared excitement, helping freshers instantly feel a part of the ChemE family.

Performances & Celebration
Annual

ChemE Night

Celebrating the spirit of Chemical Engineering.

One of the most anticipated social evenings bringing together students across batches. It celebrates the unique culture of the Chemical Engineering community through performances, interactions, and shared moments of joy beyond academics.

Traditional Evening

Where culture meets community.

A vibrant celebration of diversity where students dress in cultural attire and showcase music, dance, and traditions from across the country, reflecting the spirit of IIT Delhi's close-knit community.

Practical

Workshops

Complement classroom learning with practical exposure. From technical tools to emerging areas in chemical engineering, explore concepts through hands-on learning and develop the mindset to tackle real engineering challenges.

Research

Research Expo

Ideas, Innovation, and Inquiry.

Explore ongoing research projects and discover how fundamental scientific ideas translate into impactful innovations. An inspiring platform for those eager to step into the world of research.

Industry

Industry Visits

Bridging theory and industrial practice.

Visit large-scale chemical plants and industrial facilities. Witness firsthand how transport phenomena, reaction engineering, and process design operate at scale. Invaluable insight into real-world engineering impact.

Informal interactions with faculty members outside formal settings. Learn about research journeys, academic perspectives, and career pathways while strengthening mentor-student bonds.
