About Us

Chemical Engineering Society

IIT Delhi's premier student-led organization fostering excellence in chemical engineering

The Chemical Engineering Society (ChES), IIT Delhi is the student-led body representing the academic and professional community of the Department of Chemical Engineering. We serve as a dynamic platform that connects students with transformative opportunities in research, industry, and academic exploration beyond the classroom.

Research Excellence

Connecting students with cutting-edge research opportunities and fostering scientific innovation through faculty collaborations and advanced research initiatives.

  • Faculty research partnerships
  • Conference publications
  • Advanced research exposure

Industry Integration

Bridging the gap between academia and industry through strategic partnerships, internships, and real-world project experiences.

  • Industry-sponsored projects
  • Guest lectures & workshops
  • Career development programs

Student Development

Fostering holistic development through mentorship, leadership opportunities, and a supportive community that nurtures future engineering leaders.

  • Mentorship programs
  • Leadership development
  • Community building

Our Mission

Through initiatives such as technical workshops, research discussions, industry interactions, and mentorship-driven sessions, ChES encourages students to deepen their understanding of chemical engineering and explore emerging areas of the field. The society aims to foster curiosity, collaboration, and intellectual engagement within the department.

By bringing together students, faculty, and alumni, ChES contributes to building a strong and connected ChemE community at IIT Delhi while supporting students in their academic and professional journeys.
