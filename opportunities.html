What Can You Do as a Chemical Engineering Student?

Chemical Engineering at IIT Delhi offers far more than classroom learning. Students regularly engage in research, technical projects, competitions, and interdisciplinary collaborations that allow them to apply engineering principles to real-world challenges.

Research with Faculty

Students get the opportunity to work with faculty members on cutting-edge research problems.

Research collaboration with faculty members provides students with hands-on experience in advanced engineering challenges. These projects often span multiple disciplines and lead to meaningful contributions to the field.

Research Areas Include:

  • Sustainable energy systems
  • Advanced functional materials
  • Biotechnology and healthcare technologies
  • Process modelling and data-driven engineering
  • Environmental and climate technologies

These research projects often lead to conference presentations, publications, and advanced research opportunities.

Technical Projects

Hands-on projects are an essential part of chemical engineering training.

Process Design & Optimization

Designing and optimizing industrial chemical processes for efficiency and sustainability.

Computational Simulations

Using software tools to simulate and analyze chemical processes and systems.

Reactor Modelling

Mathematical modelling of chemical reactors and reaction kinetics.

Laboratory Research

Conducting experiments and research in state-of-the-art laboratory facilities.

These projects help students build practical engineering skills and problem-solving ability essential for their future careers.

Competitions

Students participate in national and international competitions that challenge them to apply engineering knowledge creatively.

Chemical Process Design

Designing optimal chemical processes for real-world applications.

Sustainability Challenges

Developing solutions for environmental and climate challenges.

Engineering Case Studies

Solving complex engineering problems and business cases.

Product Design

Creating innovative products and engineering solutions.

Such competitions encourage innovation, teamwork, and real-world problem solving while building a competitive edge in the engineering field.

Hackathons and Innovation Challenges

Interdisciplinary hackathons allow students to work with peers from different fields to develop technological solutions.

Hackathons provide a fast-paced environment where students collaborate across disciplines to create innovative solutions. These events foster creativity, rapid prototyping, and interdisciplinary thinking.

Focus Areas:

Energy & Sustainability
Data Science & Process Optimization
AI-driven Industrial Systems
Technology for Social Impact

Hackathons help students develop rapid prototyping, collaboration, and creative thinking skills that are valuable in modern engineering careers.

Industry Interaction and Internships

Students gain exposure to industry through various programs and experiences.

Summer Internships

Gain practical experience in industrial settings and learn how engineering concepts apply in real-world scenarios.

Industry Projects

Work on sponsored projects that address actual industry challenges and requirements.

Guest Lectures

Learn from industry experts about current trends, challenges, and opportunities in chemical engineering.

Industrial Visits

Visit manufacturing facilities, research centers, and industrial plants to understand operational processes.

These experiences help students understand how engineering concepts translate into real industrial systems and prepare them for successful careers.
